Chimichurri Sauce

Thia delightful recepy for Chimichuri Sauce is great for giving an extra zing for any meat dish and espacially grilled beef.

Ingridiants

  • 1 bundle parsleay leaves
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 1 cup of viniger
  • 1 teaspoon of Tabasco sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon of salt

Preperation

  1. peal the garlic.
  2. routhly chop the parsley. Remove most of the stocks.
  3. place the parsley and garlic in a food processor. Turn it on.
  4. while the food processor is runnig, slowly drip the oil and viniger in.
  5. pause the food processor and add salt and tobasco sauce to taste.
